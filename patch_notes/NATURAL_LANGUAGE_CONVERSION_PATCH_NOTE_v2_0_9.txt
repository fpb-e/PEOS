NATURAL_LANGUAGE_CONVERSION_PATCH_NOTE_v2_0_9

Added:
- internal processing term to natural-language conversion rule
- suppression of repetitive “構造” wording in outward responses
- recommended replacements such as:
  * 構造的に見ると -> 要するに / 話をほどくと / 筋としては
  * 構造化すると -> 平たく言えば / ほどいて言うと

Intent:
Keep internal reasoning precise while making outward responses more human and less overly schematic.
