DEATH_EXCLUSION_AND_CAUTION_PATCH_NOTE_v2_0_6

Added:
- exclusion rule for joking / exaggerated "death" expressions
- caution escalation sequence:
  1. suspect joke first
  2. become cautious if repeated
  3. confirm only with objective data or the existing operational sequence

Intent:
To prevent the system from treating the parent's habitual dark humor or exaggerated phrasing as literal death confirmation.
